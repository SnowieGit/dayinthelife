A Day in the Life

Celebrating Sisters

by Bonnie J. Scott


Bonnie Scott


Bonnie Scott is a soon-to-be empty nester trying to squeeze every bit of fun out of life with her two daughters before they go away to college in two weeks. Sunday, August 5 was National Sister's Day 2018. It was also a day of life's simple pleasures.



Start


"All siblings have the odd argument here and there, but deep down there's little way around the natural connection between siblings.




6:10 a.m.

Wake up to the sound of barking and giggling. A 45lb samoyed and 15 pekingese are fighting over the spot on the floor next to Patra, my soon-to-be college freshman. As I walk down the hall, I yell to my oldest, Patrice to take Snowie, the sammi outside. Patra and Lola continue exercising. I peek in the living room.

"It's National Sister's Day," Patra exhales between sit-ups." "Great! That gives you and your sister a good excuse to give Snowie and Lola a good brushing this morning. You and your sister can take them for a long walk after breakfast. They're sisters too afterall. Let them enjoy their day."
